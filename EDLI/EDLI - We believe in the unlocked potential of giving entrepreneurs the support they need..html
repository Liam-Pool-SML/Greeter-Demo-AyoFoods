The Entrepreneurial Development and Leadership Institute (EDLI), inspired by Frederick Douglass prepares Black, Latinx and Women entrepreneurs to succeed through a personalized business assessment process and targeted connections to an ecosystem of resources and support.

WELCOME TO EDLI
We facilitate the connectivity that is important for entrepreneurial success.

We see growing small businesses as the primary economic engine to stabilize communities and improve lives.

80%

of the equity capital needs of Black and Latinx entrepreneurs in Chicago go unmet compared to 46% of White Owned Businesses

$146M

There is a gap of $146M between supply and demand for equity capital among Black and Latinx entrepreneurship in Chicago

Too Few

There are too few equity capital providers of scale focused on entrepreneurs of color that provide the type of capital required by their businesses
