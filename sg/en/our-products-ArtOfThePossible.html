Heineken history

A perfectly brewed history.

The history of Heineken is the history of innovation in beer brewing. From 1873 when Gerard Heineken set out to create the ultimate premium lager beer, to present day under the watchful eye of master brewer Willem Van Waesberghe. Heineken’s iconic taste comes from its simplicity, quality and dedication to pure natural ingredients: the perfect medley of malt, barley, hops and our own special A-Yeast.
